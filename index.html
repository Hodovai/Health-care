Safe Haven

Safe Haven offers a unique individually tailored treatment experience that fulfils the requirements and expectations of our VIP clientele. We provide private, luxury therapeutic retreats and rehabilitation programs in Bali.

Our aim is to bring your whole being to optimum health and happiness through our exceptional holistic healthcare in absolute luxury.

Private & Luxurious

At Safe Haven, your customised healthcare program takes place in the form of a residential retreat in luxurious accommodation.

You will enjoy your own private residence, selected to suit your tastes, and completely furnished with everything you require for an exceptionally comfortable healing experience.

Bali

Also known as the Land of the Gods, Bali appeals through its sheer natural beauty of looming volcanoes and lush terraced rice fields that exude peace and serenity.

As Bali is located 8 degrees south of the equator, you will find the climate to be the typical tropical, warm and humid all year round with two main distinctive seasons: Dry Season and Rainy Season.

We encourage excursions such as Mountain climbering up Gunung Agung or Mount Batur, diving, and other outdoor activities that help to rekindle your appreciation of being part of a living, sustaining environment.

Programs

We will customise a unique holistic treatment program to suit your individual needs. Our programs combine medical review with intensive psychotherapy, bodywork, fitness, acupuncture, creative therapies, relaxation, rejuvenating meditation practices and nutrition to restore every part of you to good health.
